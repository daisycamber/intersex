Chapter 15 - Home

Thank you so much for sticking with me so far everyone. These chapters are unfinished and unedited, so I sincerley appreciate your interest and support in my autobiography. Feedback is very welcome.

Home was back with my parents in Germany. My parents were delighted to see me, though deeply upset and embarassed for what had happened to me in Alaska. My family decided it was best to keep me with them instead of somewhere else in foster care, and I implored them to keep me there as long as I could. The more I began to understand the languages they spoke the more clear to me it was what had gone wrong. By nearly thirsting and starving to death in the wild I had stunted my growth. Doctors told me thought I would spend the rest of my life looking like a four year old, in fact I had turned four over the summer where I was missing in the wild.
