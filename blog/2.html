Writing Music in Key

7/10/2019 12:20 PM

Learning to write music in key can be a difficult and daunting task. Particularily when it comes to learning major and minor chords, writing chord progressions, and adding a bassline to the track there is a major learning curve to making music. I hope to make this easier with software. As such, I created openchords.org in order to help people learn and write music in key.
