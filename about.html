About Me

My name is Jasper Holton aka Intersex. I am a musician, singer, writer, coder, animator and entreprenuer. I am currently working on several projects, including music which I am releasing through the record label group AWAL, audiovisual content for said music, this website, an autobiography, and a blog. My roots in music come from a wide variety of genres and I am interested in producing all genres of music, particularly electronic music. If you are interested in collaboration, please email me at intersexmusic(at)gmail(dot)com or contact me on social media.
