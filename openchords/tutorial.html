Tutorial

How to use OpenChords easily

Video Tutorial

Watch a video tutorial on YouTube.

Select Octave and Interval, Select a Measure

Select your octave and interval or use the default, and select a measure before or after adding a chord. You may also select an inversion, the chord length, number of notes, and add 7th, 9th and 13th if you like.

Add a Chord

Add a chord by pressing one of the roman numerals. Use the top row for major chords, and the bottom row for minor chords.

Play it Back!

Press the play button to play back your chord progression, and press the Export MIDI button to export it to MIDI. Delete chords with the delete (X) button, or double press the button to clear the chord progression.
