Artist Bio

A call to arms from musician Jasper Holton, Intersex is an electronic pop music project taking inspiration from a wide variety of genres including pop, rock, electronic, folk and classical. Jasper Holton is intersex, meaning female and male in the same body, and writes music about living as an intersex person. This music features everything from upbeat anthems to deep brooding vocals, with a variety of instruments including drums, piano, guitar, synthesizer, violin, cello, and more. Based near Seattle, Washington, Intersex is steadily rising to fame as a unique, engaging, and catchy source for new music.
