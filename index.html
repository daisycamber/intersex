Bio

A call to arms from musician Jasper Holton, Intersex is an electronic pop music project taking inspiration from a wide variety of genres including pop, rock, electronic, folk and classical. Jasper Holton is intersex, meaning female and male, and writes music about living as an intersex person. This music features everything from upbeat anthems to deep brooding vocals, with a variety of instruments including drums, piano, guitar, synthesizer, violin, cello, and more. Based near Seattle, Washington, Intersex is steadily rising to fame as a unique, engaging, and catchy source for new music.

Hi, it's Jasper Holton. Thanks for checking out my website! I have a lot of projects up here that I am working on and it's exciting to share them with you. My music is on the stores, my autobiography is live here, and I am updating my blog often with news and useful information. This website is hosted on GitHub pages and is a secure way to view my work.
